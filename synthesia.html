Tabby-Tammy


Tammy Tran's Portfolio



A creative project inspired by the medical condition where people are able to
visualize music. I had chosen a song that I liked at the time from a video game
and created a painting mural out of it. A struggle for me was making sure that the
artwork did not go into explicit territory -- as my goal was to make it abstract.

I am very proud of how this came out. It inspires me to do more abstract pieces in the future.
