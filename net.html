A choose your own adventure inspired by typical murder mysteries where an detective
investigates a murder in a mansion. The original concept orientated around murder,
but I decided to take a different direction with this particular manor primarily due
to scope purposes but also just for fun. :) I wanted to add a bit of personality and
humor in the writing and I believe I succeeded in doing so.

This was created before learning about bootstrap, but it proved to be a good way to understand using different webpages and navigating through them via links.
