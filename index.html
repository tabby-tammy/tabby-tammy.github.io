Artist Statement


Tammy Tran

DMA Major at SJSU

In my work, I make digital creations that often explore feelings and experiences that I have gone through.
Art is a way of self expression for myself, as I enjoy creating works that have a colorful cartoony vibe
that reminds me of my childhood or something more emotional that is rooted in my feelings. By going through
my everyday life and being able to return back to my childhood interests, I am able to create pieces that
reflect my interest in both my past and my current self. It is always my goal to create things I care about
personally more than anything else, as ultimately art is all about enjoying myself more than anything else.
